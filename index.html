Belgrade is colorful in the sun, gray in the rain, incredibly lively and surrounded by the Sava and Danube rivers. Cultural life can compete with that of Berlin: whether exhibitions, concerts or literary events - something is always happening in the Balkan metropolis. And Belgrade is also Zemun, the city on the Danube, which was once a border town between the Habsburg monarchy and the Ottoman Empire and is now a district of Belgrade.
